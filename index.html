Bira Barbosa

Is a lawyer, and has a master's degree in Philososy and in Human Rights. With over a decade of experience, Bira has been dedicated to delivering exceptional learning and development, organizational development (O.D.), and diversity and inclusion (D&I) services to leading companies like Calvin Klein, WestRock, Montreal Museum of Fine Arts and SAP.

As a facilitator for Catalyst, a global nonprofit committed to gender equity, Bira collaborates with top organizations to create workplaces that support and uplift women.

His work at the Montreal Fine Arts Museum involved developing and implementing a comprehensive OD and DEAI strategy, ensuring a more inclusive and effective organization.

Advogado, pós-graduado em Filosofia, cursou Business Administration na Universidade do Alabama. Formado em Coaching pela Sociedade Latino Americana de Coaching, já atuou como facilitador e treinador na Maxion, Calvin Klein, Hospira, SAP, Ernest Young e Guy Carpenter, entre outras. Coach de diversos executivos, ajuda-os na obtenção de excelência na Comunicação Apreciativa e a desenvolverem liderança intercultural.
